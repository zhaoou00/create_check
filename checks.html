This page generates checks in pdf files.

Please download this template and fill in all the information you want on the checks.

Click the button "Choose File" to select the file then hit "submit"

You can print the checks on plain letter size paper and deposit the checks with your cellphone. Just endorse the blank back of the paper.

You can also print the checks on blank check templates with a normal home printer. Use the checks anywhere you want.

I don't keep the files you upload. People receiving your checks can see those info anyway.
